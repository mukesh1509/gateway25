About Gateway

Get ready for Gateway’25, a National-Level Media Fest celebrating creativity and innovation in filmmaking!

Showcase your talent and compete for exciting prizes while gaining exposure in the media industry.

Gateway,the National-Level Media Fest organized by the Society of Media Sciences, Department of Media Sciences, Anna University, has been a beacon of creativity and excellence since its inception in 2005. With nearly two decades of rich history, Gateway has grown into a vibrant celebration of media talent, providing students with an unmatched platform to explore, express, and excel in various disciplines, including filmmaking, photography, animation, design, and advertising.Drawing over 500 entries from 200+ colleges annually and hosting more than 2000 attendees , the event stands as a testament to its enduring legacy and value in shaping the next generation of media professionals. By fostering innovation through contests, workshops, and interactive sessions, Gateway continues to uphold its mission of bridging the gap between aspiring creators and industry experts.

Over the years, Gateway has welcomed an illustrious array of icons who have significantly enriched its narrative. Visionary directors like Balu Mahendra, Rajiv Menon, Prabhu Solomon, Mohan Raja, K.V. Anand, Amir, Vetrimaran, Karthik Subbaraj, Radhakrishnan Parthiban, Balaji Mohan, Rajesh M. Selva, and Lokesh Kanagaraj have inspired participants with their groundbreaking contributions to cinema. Esteemed industry professionals, including Cartoonist Madan, Cinematographers Rathnavel and Om Prakash, Actor Vijay Sethupathi, Hip Hop Tamizha Aadhi, Neeya Naana Fame Gopinath, and wildlife filmmaker Shekar Dattatri, have shared their insights and journeys, making Gateway a crucible of learning and inspiration for aspiring media talents.

This legacy has been powered by prestigious sponsors and partners like Zee Tamil, The Hindu, Dinamalar, PVR, Inox, Behindwoods, Indiaglitz, SS Music, Suryan FM, Radio One, Radio Mirchi, Animation Express, Image Creative Education, State Bank of India, Union Bank, ONGC, LIC, Indian Oil, and the Russian Cultural Centre. Their unwavering support has helped in solidifying Gateway’s position as a premier platform, celebrating creativity and innovation while shaping the future of India’s media landscape.

Society of Media Sciences

The Society of Media Sciences (SMS) is the vibrant and creative student body of the Department of Media Sciences, Anna University. It serves as a platform for students to develop their talents, connect with industry professionals, and cultivate essential skills in leadership and teamwork. Through organizing workshops, interactive sessions, and industry-focused competitions, SMS fosters collaboration and innovation among its members. SMS is dedicated to empowering students to become confident leaders and team players, ready to excel in the dynamic world of media and communication.

The society also leads Gateway, a National Level Media Festival, the trademark event of The Society of Media Sciences, providing students with a platform to showcase their creativity while working together as a team to deliver exceptional results.

Department of Media Sciences, CEG Anna University

The Department of Media Sciences (DMS) at Anna University has been a pioneer in media education in India since its establishment in 2002. Known for its comprehensive curriculum, DMS blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience to prepare students for dynamic careers in mass communication, journalism, video and audio production, animation, design, and advertising.

The department fosters a vibrant and creative learning environment, encouraging innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills that meet the demands of today’s fast-paced media industry. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a commitment to academic excellence, DMS continues to set benchmarks in shaping the next generation of media professionals.
